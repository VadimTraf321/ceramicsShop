Features of using ceramic cookware in the oven?
Use ceramic products only for their intended purpose. Cooking utensils can be used in electric and gas ovens. But it is recommended to place ceramic dishes in a minimally heated oven and gradually heat them to the temperature required for

It is not recommended to expose ceramic products to sudden temperature changes, i.e. Dishes containing frozen or refrigerated food should not be placed directly from the refrigerator into a hot oven.

It is strictly forbidden to place ceramic coffee pots or teapots on electric stoves or open flames, to use electric heating devices such as “boilers” to boil water, or to heat the contents in microwave or electric ovens.
How can you update your dishes?
I also want to tell you that if you constantly use dishes, their appearance ages over time, the bottoms get rubbed, the cracks are stained with tea. If you want to update your dishes after a year or two, this is quite possible. If you burn such aged dishes in a muffle furnace at the right temperature, all marks, abrasions and odors will be removed from it. If desired, you can coat the dishes again with a transparent glaze and fire them again. After this, your favorite set will be as good as new and will last just as long! The only inconvenience is that you will have to send the dishes back and forth. If you are interested in the possibility of updating your dishes, you can contact our managers about this issue.
